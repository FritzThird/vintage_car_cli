Carolyn Cline Music

                  

 

Carolyn Cline is a world class preformer who has traveled the world performing her unique and excitng show.  She is a veteran of Las Vegas, Hollywood, and New York.  Carolyn has also perfomed internationally in Tokyo, Japan , Bangkok, Thailand , Manila, Philippines , and Papeete, Tahiti are just a few.  Having been in the entertainment business for over thirty years, Carolyn has appeared with such mega stars as Bob Hope, Red Skelton, Liberace, Tony Orlando, Lee Greenwood, and Les Elgart's Orchrestra.  Carolyn has earned the titles for Miss Florida, 1978, Miss America 1979 2nd runner up, Miss New Mexico Universe 1973 in the Miss USA Pageant and she won the talent and swimsuit award at the national pageants.  She is a talented and versatile performer who plays a variety of instruments including the piano, organ, guitar, banjo, mandalin, flute, violin, and drums.  Carolyn is a musician, singer, opening act, entertainer, and composer.  She has earned a Bachelor of Music Degree and a Master of Arts Degree in Music Education.  Carolyn also sings a tribute to her late great cousin Patsy Cline.  Here are the memories of a talented entertainer named Patsy Cline from Winchester, Virginia, who still manages to capture the hearts of everyone through Carolyn Cline's voice, phrasing, and style to an unforgettable artist.
